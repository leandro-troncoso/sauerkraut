SAUERKRAUT was an app project developed for employees of a German restaurant. This task included branding, UX/UI design and app deisgn.



The app allows for saving the restaurant's recipes for reference at any time, covering both the mise-en-place process and the dispatch and plating process. Additionally, it has an alarm system for recipes' various processes.



This app would allow for orderly managment of a highly demanding gastronomic service and proper time managment, avoiding delays or issues in dish preparation.
